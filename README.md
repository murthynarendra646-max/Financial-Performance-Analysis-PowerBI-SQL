# Financial-Performance-Analysis-PowerBI-SQL
A comprehensive Financial &amp; Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to provide a high-level managerial overview of business performance. This project transforms raw data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

# Business Insights
The business operates at a moderate profit margin (~11.6%), indicating room for optimization in pricing or cost structure
Sales show a consistent upward trend across months, but profit growth is not proportional, suggesting inefficiencies in cost or discounting
Technology category contributes the highest profit, making it the primary revenue driver
Furniture category generates the lowest profit, indicating potential inefficiency or low demand
Central region is the top-performing region in terms of profit, followed by North and North Asia
The discount vs profit analysis shows a negative correlation, where higher discounts lead to reduced profitability
From AI insights (Key Influencers):
When sales exceed a certain threshold (~464+), profit significantly increases
Certain regions (especially Central) contribute disproportionately to overall sales and profit

# Business Recommendations
Focus on increasing sales volume beyond key thresholds to maximize profit growth
Optimize or reduce discount strategies, especially in low-margin scenarios
Invest more in high-performing regions like Central and North to scale revenue
Reassess Furniture category performance and consider cost reduction or repositioning strategies
Improve profit margins by controlling cost structure, as sales growth alone is not translating fully into profit
Use AI insights to prioritize high-impact segments instead of treating all regions equally
